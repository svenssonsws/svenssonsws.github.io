Zhou Zishu

& Wen Kexing

Synopsis

Skylight leader Zhou Zishu (Zhang zhehan) quit skylight organization at the cost of his life in order to seek freedom.

When Zhou Zishu's life is just around the corner, he meets Wen Kexing (Gong Jun), the owner of ghost Valley, who is determined to destroy the world and want revenge.

They get to know each other because they are involved in the disputes in the river and lake, and finally become friends of each other's salvation. A conspiracy, two confidants, five pieces of treasure puzzle, twenty years of gratitude and resentment, and thousands of years of dream of the river and lake make their fate closely linked
